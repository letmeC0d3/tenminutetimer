Cómo usar el temporizador de 5 min

To start your **five minute timer countdown**, just:

  1. Presionar el botón central de **Iniciar**.
  2. Choose from relaxing background sounds like **Rain** or **Olas del Océano** if you are meditating or taking a break.
  3. Wait for the timer to reach 00:00. Once it finishes, the screen will flash and your selected alarm (e.g. Campana Cálida) will trigger.
