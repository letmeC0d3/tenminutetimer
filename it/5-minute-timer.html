Come utilizzare il timer da 5 min

To start your **five minute timer countdown**, just:

  1. Clicca sul tasto centrale **Avvia**.
  2. Choose from relaxing background sounds like **Rain** or **Onde dell'Oceano** if you are meditating or taking a break.
  3. Wait for the timer to reach 00:00. Once it finishes, the screen will flash and your selected alarm (e.g. Campanella Dolce) will trigger.
