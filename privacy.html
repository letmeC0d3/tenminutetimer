Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 9, 2026

Welcome to TenMinuteTimer.com. Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This policy describes how we collect, use, and process data. In short: **we do not track, collect, or monetize your personal information.** All operations run locally inside your web browser.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Because TenMinuteTimer.com is a client-side utility, we do not require you to register for an account, log in, or provide personal identifiers. When you use the timer:

  • We do NOT collect your name, email address, physical location, or IP address.
  • We do NOT track the durations, settings, or audio profiles you set on the timer.
  • No tracking parameters or cookies are set for marketing purposes.

2. Local Storage Usage

We use local browser storage (specifically `localStorage`) to store your aesthetic preference: your selected theme (Light Mode or Dark Mode). This data is stored entirely on your computer's local hard drive and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

3. Third-Party Services & Links

We may include links to third-party websites or services. If you click on a link to a third-party site, you will be directed to that site. Note that these external sites are not operated by us, and we strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of those websites. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

4. Children's Privacy

Our website is designed to be child-friendly and classroom-safe. Since we collect zero personal data, our service is fully compliant with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and GDPR regulations regarding children's privacy.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy practices, feel free to visit our Contact Page and drop us a message.
